unJobless is an AI-powered no bs, open-sourced simple yet straightforward and powerful mock interview platform designed to help students and job seekers practice for real interviews. Users select a role such as Software Engineer, Consultant, Data Analyst, or UX Designer receive randomised interview questions tailored to that field. They type their answer, submit that answer and receive instant feedback on the quality and depth of understanding of their response, along with a running score. MockMate helps you build confidence and get comfortable answering tough questions before the real thing.
